beSide - HackSingapore 2024

Building stronger neighborhood connections through technology.

Introduction

beSide is a platform developed during HackSingapore 2024 with the aim of fostering deeper connections within local neighborhoods. It allows residents to offer or request help, register their skills, and collaborate on community projects. Our mission is to encourage active community involvement and build a more connected and supportive local community.

Technological Overview

Built using PHP and the Laravel framework, beSide is supported by a MySQL database, ensuring secure and efficient data management. The combination of a robust backend and intuitive user interface allows seamless interactions, making it easier for residents to connect with each other and participate in local activities.

Key Features

  • Request & Offer Help: Users can reach out for assistance or extend their skills to those in need.
  • Skill Registration: Residents can register their skills, making it easier for others to find and collaborate with them.
  • Community Projects: A space for residents to propose, join, and contribute to local projects, fostering a collaborative environment.

The Impact

beSide aims to bring residents closer, making neighborhoods more vibrant and cohesive. Whether through organizing local clean-up events or helping a neighbor in need, this platform nurtures a spirit of togetherness and mutual support.
