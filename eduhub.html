A Tailored Learning Management System for Schools

Designed with a focus on simplifying online education, EduHub bridges the gap between students and teachers, making learning truly engaging and efficient.

Introduction

Welcome to EduHub! This project was all about creating a user-friendly and effective platform that integrates various aspects of online learning. Inspired by platforms like Moodle, EduHub goes a step further by offering custom features to better suit the dynamic needs of modern classrooms.
